Operating hours & fares

Generally 06:00–20:00. Minimum fare MYR 2.00, increases by distance — state your boarding point when asking the driver. Always confirm operating hours with your driver.

Route accuracy

Complete = confirmed end-to-end. Partial = observed at least twice, not fully confirmed. Undef = single observation only. Always double-check with the driver.

Disclaimer

Routes are community-observed, not official. Google Maps may reroute during peak hours. Bus schedules are not fixed — buses generally depart when full.
